Nature's daily protection

Powerful plant compounds from high-quality olives.

Our extra virgin olive oil is naturally rich in polyphenols, powerful plant compounds found in high-quality olives. Polyphenols are often called antioxidants because they help protect the body from oxidative stress.

Oxidative stress is a natural process that happens in the body every day, but too much of it can damage cells, fats, and blood vessels over time.

The accurate message

Polyphenols help protect and support the body every day. In Europe, olive oil polyphenols are officially recognised for helping protect blood lipids from oxidative stress when the oil provides enough hydroxytyrosol and related compounds.
